Trade in your wallet instead of here

Or you can just do this in your wallet.

How to buy in wallet

  • Find symbol you want to buy & click "Get address"
  • Go to your wallet and find aUSD token, click "Send"
  • Paste in the address for receiver.
  • Note: If you can, save the address to your contact list for easy access next time
  • Type in the amount & click Confirm - we will now buy your symbol, don't forget to add it to wallet.

Sell your securities

You sell the stocks using your wallet

  • Find the token you want so sell in you wallet and click Send
  • Put the in the aUSD address
  • Remember to save it in your contact list, for easy access next time
  • Type in the amount & click Confirm
